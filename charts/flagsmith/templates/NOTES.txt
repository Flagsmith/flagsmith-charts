######################################################
#####  In-chart InfluxDB has been removed.       #####
######################################################

If you were using the chart-provisioned InfluxDB instance,
it is no longer deployed. Your options for analytics are:

  1. External InfluxDB: set `influxdbExternal.enabled` to `true`
     and configure connection details under the `influxdbExternal` key.
  2. PostgreSQL: set `UsePostgresForAnalytics.enabled` to `true`
     (requires `taskProcessor.enabled` to also be `true`).

To migrate existing analytics data from InfluxDB to PostgreSQL,
enable `jobs.migrateAnalyticsData`.
